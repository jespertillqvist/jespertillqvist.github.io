About Jesper Tillqvist

Designing, making and restoring woodwind instruments was where I began my career, learning the intricacies of precious metalwork and precision engineering through all stages of the creative workflow.

These skills led me to the fine jewellery industry where I learned to interpret the needs of clients into beautiful, one off pieces.

Running parallel has always been my interest in technology and developing skills in coding, web design and 3D modelling.

Combining jewellery making and design into work with CAD was a natural and fulfilling career step for me.
